What is orchitis？

Orchitis is usually caused by bacteria and viruses.Experts pointed out that bacterial infections rarely occur in the testicles themselves. This is because the testicles have a rich supply of blood and lymph and are highly resistant to bacterial infections. Bacterial orchitis is mostly caused by inflammation of the adjacent epididymis, so it is also called epididymis-orchitis.
